# HDFC Life Claim Procedure

## Death Claim Steps

- Notify HDFC Life through the branch, call center, or online claim intimation form.
- Keep the policy number, claimant identity, and date of death ready.
- Submit the required document set and bank details for payout.

## Settlement Window

- Once documents are complete, the insurer should process the claim promptly and keep the nominee informed of any missing items.
