# LIC Death Claim Procedure

## How to File a Death Claim with LIC

- Inform the nearest LIC branch and share the policy number and date of death.
- Collect the claim form and fill nominee details carefully.
- Submit the complete claim set at the servicing branch.
- Track the claim until settlement confirmation is received.

## Settlement Window

- LIC should settle a standard death claim within 30 days after receiving all required documents.
