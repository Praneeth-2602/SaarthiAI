# LIC Documents Required for Death Claim

- Death certificate
- LIC death claim form signed by nominee
- Original policy bond or policy schedule copy
- Nominee Aadhaar or other identity proof
- Nominee address proof
- Cancelled cheque or bank passbook copy
- NEFT mandate details

## Additional Cases

- Hospital records if the death happened soon after policy issue
- FIR and post-mortem documents for accidental death
