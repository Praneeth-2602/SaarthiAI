# Insurance Grievance Process

## First Level

- Raise the issue with the insurer's branch or customer care team.
- Keep the complaint number and all submission receipts.

## Second Level

- If the insurer does not resolve the issue, escalate through the insurer's grievance officer.
- Mention the policy number, claim date, and copies of all submitted documents.

## IRDAI

- A nominee can escalate unresolved claim delays through IRDAI's grievance channel if the insurer does not respond within the expected timeframe.
