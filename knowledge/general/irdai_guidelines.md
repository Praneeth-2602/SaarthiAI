# IRDAI Claim Settlement Guidelines

## Standard Death Claim Timeline

- Insurers should acknowledge the claim and request any missing documents quickly.
- Once all required documents are received, a standard death claim should usually be settled within 30 days.
- If the insurer needs further investigation, it should communicate that clearly and still complete the process within the regulatory timeline.
- Delays should be explained in writing to the nominee.

## Common Documents

- Death certificate
- Claim form signed by the nominee
- Original policy bond or policy schedule copy
- Nominee identity proof
- Bank account proof for settlement
